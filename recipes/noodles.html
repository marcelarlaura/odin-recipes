Buttered Noodles

Buttered noodles are simple to make with your favorite pasta, butter, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper for a quick and easy, kid-friendly dish.

Fresh herbs and a little lemon juice could be added to amp up the flavor. Perfect to serve either as-is or alongside steak, chicken, or meatballs.

It's such a delicious recipe, yet I get many questions on how to make it.

Ingredients:

  • Any type of noodles

  • Butter

  • Grated parmesan cheese

  • Salt

  • Ground black pepper

Steps:

  1. Boil the noodles in slightly salted water until it's tender, yet still firm

  2. Drain the noodles in a colander, then return it to the pot

  3. Mix butter, cheese, salt and pepper into the cooked noodles until all ingredients are evenly combined
