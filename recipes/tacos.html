Chicken Tacos

These easy slow cooker chicken tacos are easy to make with just 3 ingredients in your crockpot.

Spoon the filling into warm tortillas for a very tasty meal any day of the week.

Ingredients:

  • Broth
  • Taco seasoning
  • Chicken

Steps:

  1. Mix chicken broth and taco seasoning in a bowl
  2. Pour chicken broth over the chicken and then place it in a slow cooker
  3. Cook on low for 6 to 8 hours. Then shred the chicken
